THE REBBE SHLITA

Everyone has the possibility to contact; The Rebbe advises his own way. At any given moment, anyone can write a letter to the Rebbe and ask for an advice or for a blessing.

The Lubavitcher Rebbe's inspiration encourages us to spread the Seven Noahide Laws throughout the world. Its objective: to add values of justice, honesty and peace to the world. In the writings of our Sage, the Rambam, "Moses our Teacher received the commandment from G-d to encourage the observance of these basic laws by all of humanity so that they may come to keep the commandments that Noah was instructed to observe".

  • Without hunger
  • Without wars
  • Without competition
  • A world of peace
